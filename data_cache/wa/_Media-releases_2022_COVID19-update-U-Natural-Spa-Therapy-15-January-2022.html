COVID-19 update - U Natural Spa Therapy - 15 January 2022

Anyone who attended U Natural Spa Therapy in Applecross between 7 and 13 January 2022 is required to immediately get tested for COVID-19 and to quarantine for 14 days from their last exposure. These people should also ring 13COVID (1326843).

This location has been visited by multiple COVID cases.

In the seven day-window, only 11 people used the SafeWA app to register their attendance at U Natural Spa and Therapy.

WA Health believes dozens of other people would have attended this business during this time.

This is a significant exposure risk.

Given the timeframe, WA Health needs to urgently speak with positive cases to identify further potential exposure sites in the coming days.

Exposure sites will continue to be updated on the HealthyWA website. People are encouraged to check this site regularly.
