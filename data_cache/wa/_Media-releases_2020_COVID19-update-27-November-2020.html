COVID-19 update – 27 November 2020

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia.

The State’s total number of cases remains at 817. WA Health is monitoring 30 active cases and 778 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 802 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 797 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 546,698 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 90,424 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
