COVID-19 update – 29 November 2020

The Department of Health has today reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia. The case is in hotel quarantine.

The State’s total number of cases now stands at 818. WA Health is monitoring 24 active cases and 785 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 690 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 687 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 546,698 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 90,424 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
