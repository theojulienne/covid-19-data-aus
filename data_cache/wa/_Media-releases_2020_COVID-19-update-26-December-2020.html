COVID-19 update – 26 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported six new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia, bringing the State’s total to 854.

The confirmed cases are all in hotel quarantine. Four cases are female, two male – all related to overseas travel.

WA Health is monitoring 13 active cases and 832 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 452 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 452 were assessed and 451 swabbed.

There have been 612,889 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 99,437 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
