COVID-19 update – 23 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight.

However, three historical cases have been reported, bringing the State’s total to 654.

There are now 14 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia. To date 631 people have recovered from the virus.

Yesterday 736 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 709 were assessed and 705 were swabbed.

There have been 232,977 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 39,777 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA for the latest information on COVID-19.
