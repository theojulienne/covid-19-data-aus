COVID-19 update – 10 August 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 642.

There are three active cases in the State – two Western Australians and one from interstate. They are all in hotel quarantine.

To date, 630 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 414 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 405 were assessed and 402 were swabbed.

There have been 274,560 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 46,182 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website (external site) for the latest information on COVID-19.
