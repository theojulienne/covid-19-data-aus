COVID-19 update – 21 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight. The State total stands at 651.

There are 17 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia. To date 625 people have now recovered from the virus.

Yesterday 853 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 814 were assessed and 808 were swabbed.

There have been 227,546 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 38,865 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA for the latest information on COVID-19.
