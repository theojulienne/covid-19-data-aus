COVID-19 update – 6 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported two new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia.

The confirmed cases – two men in their 50s – travelled to Perth from overseas. They are in hotel quarantine.

The State’s total number of cases is 830.

WA Health is monitoring 13 active cases and 808 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 510 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 508 were assessed and swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.

Media contact: 9222 4333
Follow us on Twitter: @WAHealth

An updated breakdown of cumulative COVID-19 tests performed in WA will be released on Monday.
