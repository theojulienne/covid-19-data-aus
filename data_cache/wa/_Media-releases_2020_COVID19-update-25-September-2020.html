COVID-19 update – 25 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total remains at 668. There are nine active cases being monitored.

To date, 650 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 476 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 459 were assessed and 456 swabbed.

There have been 410,171 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 70,430 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
