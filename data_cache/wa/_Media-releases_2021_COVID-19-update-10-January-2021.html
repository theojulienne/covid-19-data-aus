COVID-19 update – 10 January 2021

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia. The State’s total remains at 875.

WA Health is monitoring 15 active cases and 851 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 2,739 people presented to WA Health-managed COVID clinics – 2,713 were assessed and 2,707 were swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website (external site) for the latest information on COVID-19.

Media contact: 9222 4333
Follow us on Twitter: @WAHealth

NB: An updated breakdown of cumulative COVID-19 tests performed in WA will be released on Monday.
