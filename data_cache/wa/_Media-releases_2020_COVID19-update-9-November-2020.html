COVID-19 update – 9 November 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s positive COVID-19 case total stands at 776. WA Health is monitoring 10 active cases.

To date 757 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus.

Yesterday 534 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 523 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 506,297 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 84,986 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
