COVID-19 update – 22 June 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new active cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia today.

The State’s total stands at 605.

There are three active cases in State and 593 people have recovered from the virus in WA.  

Yesterday 477 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 461 were assessed and 459 were swabbed.

To date there have been 162,065 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 28,681 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
