COVID-19 update – 10 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia.

The State’s total number of cases remains at 832.

WA Health is monitoring 12 active cases and 811 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 704 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 694 were assessed and 693 were swabbed.

There have been 579,372 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 95,594 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
