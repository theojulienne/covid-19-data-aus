COVID 19 update 26 November 2020

The Department of Health has today reported seven new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia.

The confirmed cases – males aged between 36 and 58 years – travelled to Perth from overseas. They are all in hotel quarantine.

The State’s total number of cases is now 817. WA Health is monitoring 32 active cases and 776 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 903 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 890 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 543,961 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 90,006 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
