COVID-19 update – 22 July 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 overnight. The State total stands at 651.

There are 16 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia. To date 626 people have recovered from the virus.

Yesterday 720 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 695 were assessed and 688 were swabbed.

There have been 230,402 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 39,368 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA for the latest information on COVID-19.
