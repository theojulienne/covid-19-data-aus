COVID 19 update 21 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia, bringing the State’s total to 846.

The confirmed case is a woman in her 40s who travelled to Perth from overseas. She is in hotel quarantine. 

WA Health is monitoring 11 active cases and 826 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 617 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 613 were assessed and 612 were swabbed.

There have been 606, 280 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 98,602 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
