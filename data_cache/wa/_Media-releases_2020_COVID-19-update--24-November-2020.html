COVID 19 update 24 November 2020

The Department of Health has today reported four new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia.

The confirmed cases – three females and one male aged between 27 and 67 – travelled to Perth from overseas. They are all in hotel quarantine. 

The State’s total number of cases is now 808. WA Health is monitoring 25 active cases and 774 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA. 

Yesterday 899 people presented to WA COVID clinics -- 879 were assessed and 878 swabbed.

There have been 539,122 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 89,334 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.

Media contact: 9222 4333

Follow us on Twitter: @WAHealth

An updated breakdown of cumulative COVID-19 tests performed in WA will be released on Monday.
