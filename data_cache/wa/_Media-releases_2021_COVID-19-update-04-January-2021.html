COVID-19 update – 04 January 2021

The Department of Health has today reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia, bringing the State’s total to 868.

The new case is a male in his 40s who has returned from overseas and is in hotel quarantine.

WA Health is monitoring 17 active cases and 842 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

There have been 636,386 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 101,858 were from regional WA.

Yesterday 1,927 people presented to WA Health managed COVID clinics – 1,913 were assessed and 1,908 were swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
