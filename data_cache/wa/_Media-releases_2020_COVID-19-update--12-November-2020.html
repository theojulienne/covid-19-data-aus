COVID 19 update 12 November 2020

The Department of Health has reported three new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The confirmed cases – two females and one male aged in their 30s and 40s – returned to Perth from overseas. They are in hotel quarantine.

The State’s positive COVID-19 case total now stands at 783.

WA Health is monitoring 14 active cases and 760 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 438 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 427 were assessed and 426 were swabbed.

There have been 512,756 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 85,911 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
