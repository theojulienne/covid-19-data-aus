COVID-19 update – 5 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported three new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia.

The confirmed cases – two men and one woman aged between 30 and 50 – travelled to Perth from overseas. They are all in hotel quarantine.

The State’s total number of cases is 828.

WA Health is monitoring 14 active cases and 805 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 988 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 982 were assessed and 979 swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.

Media contact: 9222 4333
Follow us on Twitter: @WAHealth

An updated breakdown of cumulative COVID-19 tests performed in WA will be released on Monday.
