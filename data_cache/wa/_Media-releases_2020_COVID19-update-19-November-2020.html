COVID-19 update – 19 November 2020

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia. The State’s total remains at 794.

WA Health is monitoring 18 active cases and 767 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 667 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 645 were assessed and 643 were swabbed.

There have been 527,624 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 87,815 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
