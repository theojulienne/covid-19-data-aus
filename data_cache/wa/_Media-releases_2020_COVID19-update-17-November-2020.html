COVID-19 update – 17 November 2020

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia.

The State’s positive COVID-19 case total stands at 788. WA Health is monitoring 12 active cases and 767 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 1,279 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 1,157 were assessed and 1,156 were swabbed.

There have been 521,971 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 87,004 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
