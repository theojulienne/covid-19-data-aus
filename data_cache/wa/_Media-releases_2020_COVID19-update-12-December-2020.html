COVID-19 update – 12 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia, bringing the State’s total to 835.

The confirmed case – a child – travelled to Perth from overseas with their family. They are in hotel quarantine. 

WA Health is monitoring 11 active cases and 815 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 930 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 920 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 581,566 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 95,915 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
