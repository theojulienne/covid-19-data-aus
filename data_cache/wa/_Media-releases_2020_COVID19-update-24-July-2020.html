COVID-19 update – 24 July 2020

The Department of Health is reporting no new cases of COVID-19 for the sixth day straight.

The State total number of infections stands at 654.

There are now 12 active COVID-19 cases in Western Australia. To date 633 people have recovered from the virus.

Yesterday 735 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 696 were assessed and 688 were swabbed.

There have been 235,512 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 40,121 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA (external site) for the latest information on COVID-19.
