COVID-19 update – 25 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia, bringing the State’s total to 848.

The confirmed case is a female in her 40s who returned to Perth from overseas. She is in hotel quarantine.

WA Health is monitoring seven active cases and 832 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 607 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 601 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 612,889 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 99,437 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
