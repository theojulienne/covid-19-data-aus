COVID-19 update – 21 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total remains at 662. There are three active cases being monitored.

To date, 650 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 490 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 482 were assessed and 480 swabbed.

There have been 398,198 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 68,402 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
