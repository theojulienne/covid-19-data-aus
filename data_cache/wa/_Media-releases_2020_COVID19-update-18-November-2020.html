COVID-19 update – 18 November 2020

The Department of Health has today reported six new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia, bringing the State’s total to 794.

All six confirmed cases are returned travellers who are in hotel quarantine. The five males and one female are aged between 5 and 32.

WA Health is monitoring 18 active cases and 767 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 811 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 802 were assessed and 800 were swabbed.

There have been 525,177 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 87,452 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
