COVID-19 update â€“ 9 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia. 

The confirmed case is a male teenager who travelled to Perth from overseas. He is in hotel quarantine. 

The State’s total number of cases is 832. 

WA Health is monitoring 13 active cases and 810 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA. 

Yesterday 889 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 875 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 576, 673 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 95,133 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
