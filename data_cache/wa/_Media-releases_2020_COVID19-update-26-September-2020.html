26 September 2020

COVID-19 update – 26 September 2020

The Department of Health has reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight related to overseas travel. The case is in hotel quarantine.

The State’s total now stands at 669. There are ten active cases being monitored.

To date, 650 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 707 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 696 were assessed and 689 swabbed.

There have been 410,171 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 70,430 were from regional WA*.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19. 

*Note: Total test numbers will next be updated on Tuesday due to the long weekend.
