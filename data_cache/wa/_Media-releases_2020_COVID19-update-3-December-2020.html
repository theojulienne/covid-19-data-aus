COVID-19 update – 3 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia. The State’s total number of cases remains at 823.

WA Health is monitoring 16 active cases and 798 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 680 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 674 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 561,419 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 92,284 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
