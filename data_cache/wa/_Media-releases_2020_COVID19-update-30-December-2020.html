COVID-19 update – 30 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported zero new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia, with the State’s total remaining at 859.

WA Health is monitoring 12 active cases and 838 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 1,416 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 1,409 were assessed and 1,408 were swabbed.

There have been 623,685 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 100,534 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
