COVID 19 update 30 November 2020

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia.

The State’s total number of cases remains at 818. WA Health is monitoring 21 active cases and 788 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 731 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 722 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 553,509 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 91,210 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
