04 December 2020

COVID-19 update â€“ 4 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported two new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia.

The confirmed cases are a woman in her 30s and a man in his 40s who travelled to Perth from overseas. They are both in hotel quarantine.

The State’s total number of cases is 825.

WA Health is monitoring 14 active cases and 802 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 516 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 509 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 563,757 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 92,647ff were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
