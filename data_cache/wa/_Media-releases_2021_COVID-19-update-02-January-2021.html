02 January 2021

COVID-19 update – 02 January 2021

The Department of Health has today reported three new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia, with the State’s total now standing at 866.

The three cases comprised a mother and her child, as well as one other female. All are in hotel quarantine.

WA Health is monitoring 19 active cases and 838 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 2,200 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 2,182 were assessed and 2,179 were swabbed.

There have been 626,447 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 100,919 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
