COVID-19 update – 27 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported two new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia, bringing the State's total to 855.

The confirmed cases, a man in his 40’s and a woman in her 20’s, are both in hotel quarantine and related to overseas travel.

WA Health is monitoring 13 active cases and 832 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 662 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 656 were assessed and swabbed.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.

Media contact: 9222 4333
Follow us on Twitter: @WAHealth

Note: An updated breakdown of cumulative COVID-19 tests performed in WA will be released early next week.
