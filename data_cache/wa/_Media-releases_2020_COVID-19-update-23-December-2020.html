COVID-19 update - 23 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia. The State’s total remains at 846.

WA Health is monitoring 9 active cases and 828 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA. 

Yesterday 757 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 733 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 609,886 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 99,116 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
