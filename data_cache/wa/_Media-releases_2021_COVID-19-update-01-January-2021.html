COVID-19 update – 01 January 2021

The Department of Health has today reported two new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia, with the State’s total now standing at 863.

One new case is a female, the other a male, both who are in hotel quarantine and who have returned to Australia from international travel.

WA Health is monitoring 16 active cases and 838 people have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 895 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 879 were assessed and 877 were swabbed.

There have been 626,447 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 100,919 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
