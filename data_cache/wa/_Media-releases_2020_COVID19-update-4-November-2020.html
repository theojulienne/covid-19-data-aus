COVID-19 update – 4 November 2020

The Department of Health has reported one new case of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The confirmed case is a male traveller in his 30s who returned to Perth from overseas. He is in hotel quarantine.

The State’s positive COVID-19 case total stands at 771. To date 748 people have recovered from the virus.

WA Health is monitoring 14 active cases, including one crew member from the AL Messilah livestock carrier which remains berthed at Fremantle Port.

The AL Messilah and the Key Integrity are scheduled to clear quarantine on 5 November.

Yesterday 730 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 719 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 497,944 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 83,806 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
