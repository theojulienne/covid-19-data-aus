COVID-19 update – 11 August 2020

The Department of Health has reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia overnight.

The State’s total stands at 642.

There are three active cases in the State – two Western Australians and one from interstate.

To date, 630 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 899 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 872 were assessed and 866 were swabbed.

There have been 276,579 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 46,394 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website (external site) for the latest information on COVID-19.
