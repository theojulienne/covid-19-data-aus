COVID-19 update – 20 November 2020

The Department of Health has today reported two new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia, bringing the State’s total to 796.

The two confirmed cases are a male and a female, both in their 60s, who have returned from overseas. They are in hotel quarantine.

WA Health is monitoring 20 active cases and 767 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA.

Yesterday 529 people presented to WA COVID-19 clinics – 517 were assessed and 516 were swabbed.

There have been 529,780 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 88,166 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
