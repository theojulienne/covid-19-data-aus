COVID 19 update 7 December 2020

The Department of Health has today reported no new cases of COVID-19 in Western Australia. 

The State’s total number of cases remains at 830. 

WA Health is monitoring 13 active cases and 808 confirmed cases have recovered from the virus in WA. 

Yesterday 611 people presented to WA COVID clinics – 608 were assessed and swabbed.

There have been 571,245 COVID-19 tests performed in WA. Of those tested, 94,478 were from regional WA.

Visit WA Health’s HealthyWA website for the latest information on COVID-19.
