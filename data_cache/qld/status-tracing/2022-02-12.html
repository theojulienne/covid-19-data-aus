Data caveats

We make every effort to ensure this data is accurate at time of publication. It is point-in-time data.

There may be a lag in reporting of particular data due to data quality assurance activities, ongoing maintenance or other factors.

Data is subject to change as additional information is obtained.

COVID-19 case summary:

Data presented in this report are updated daily, and are as at 7pm the previous day.

Total cases
Total number of COVID-19 cases in Queensland during the pandemic.
Last 24 hours
Number of COVID-19 cases in Queensland in the 24 hours to 7pm yesterday.
Active cases
Number of people who have tested positive to COVID-19 in the past 7 days.
Current hospitalisations
Number of people currently receiving treatment for COVID-19 in a Queensland hospital.
Patients current in ICU
Number of people currently receiving treatment for COVID-19 in a Queensland hospital intensive care unit (ICU).
Deaths

Total number of people who have tested positive for COVID-19 around the time of their death.

As it can be difficult to attribute a death to a single cause, particularly for people who have multiple medical conditions, deaths include anyone who dies with COVID-19, except where COVID-19 can be easily excluded as a factor in the death (e.g. a vehicle accident).

Cases of First Nations people
Total number of COVID-19 cases in Queensland during the pandemic in persons who have identified as an Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander person.

Case data by Hospital and Health Service (HHS) and Local Government Area (LGA):

Location data (by HHS or by LGA) should be interpreted with caution. In most cases, location is derived using the residential address when that case was notified, but may alternately have been derived from the location of the Public Health Unit managing the case, or the location where the test was ordered.

Vaccination summary:

This data reflects the COVID-19 vaccine doses administered by Queensland Health.

Data presented in this report are updated daily, and are as at 4pm the previous day.

Vaccines are also administered by general practice, in pharmacies, and in residential aged care and disability care facilities.

Last 24 hours

Number of vaccine doses administered by Queensland Health in the 24 hours to 4pm yesterday. Includes first, second and booster doses.

Total doses

Total number of vaccine doses administered by Queensland Health. Includes first, second and booster doses.

Testing:

Testing refers only to tests completed by Queensland Health or by private pathology providers. Most are PCR tests, however point-of-care Rapid Antigen Tests administered at Queensland Health testing clinics may also be included.
